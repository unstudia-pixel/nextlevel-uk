Andúril - Flame of the West. Reforged from the shards of Narsil, the sword of Elendil, this is the blade that was broken and reforged to be worthy of the heir of Isildur. This is Aragorn's iconic weapon from The Lord of the Rings, now available as a full-scale metal replica.

Crafted from 440 stainless steel with a hand-engraved blade featuring the Elvish runes: "Nányë Andúril i né Narsil i macil Elendilo. Lercuvanten i móli Mordoreo." (I am Andúril who was Narsil, the sword of Elendil. The slaves of Mordor shall flee from me.) The leather-wrapped grip and detailed pommel mirror the film prop with exceptional accuracy.

This is a blunt-edged display piece designed for collectors and enthusiasts. It is not designed or intended for combat use. The included hardwood wall plaque means it's ready to mount the moment it arrives - no additional hardware needed.

All metal sword purchases require proof of age (18+) prior to dispatch. Our team will contact you to confirm before shipping.

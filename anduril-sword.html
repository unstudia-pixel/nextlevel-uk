Common Questions

Collector Q&A

Will the Elvish runes tarnish or fade over time?
No. The runes are deep-etched directly into the 440 stainless steel - not painted or printed. This means they will not tarnish, fade, or wear away over time. The etch depth is the same grade used on premium commemorative blades.
Does it come ready to hang, or do I need extra fittings?
Hang-ready straight out of the box. The hardwood wall plaque includes all mounting brackets and wall fixings. You need a screwdriver - nothing else. Takes about 5 minutes from unboxing to mounted on the wall.
Is the wall plaque solid hardwood or MDF?
Solid hardwood - not MDF or composite. The plaque is weight-rated to hold the full 2.1kg sword safely. You will feel the difference the moment you lift it out of the box.
How does the 18+ ID verification work exactly?
After placing your order, our team emails you within 2 hours. Simply reply with a photo of your passport, driving licence, or any government-issued ID showing your date of birth. Once confirmed we pack and dispatch within 48 hours. Your ID is used solely for age verification and is never stored beyond that check.
Is the blade sharp? Is it safe to handle and display around children?
Fully blunt edge. This is an ornamental display piece - completely safe to handle. The edge is deliberately finished to a rounded profile for collector safety. It is not a weapon and is sold strictly as a display replica. We do however recommend keeping all metal replicas out of reach of young children as standard practice.
